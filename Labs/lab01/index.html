Authentic New Orleans Gumbo

by Lauren Allen

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • All-Purpose Flour
  1. Combine the flour and oil in a large pot over medium low heat.
  2. Mix flour-oil mixture consistently until it forms a dark roux.
  3. In a separate pan, cook the sausage and the chicken thighs.
  4. Add diced vegetables to the roux and stir until fragrant.
  5. Add seasoning and chicken stock; stir until combined.
  6. Add the cooked sausage and chicken to the pot and stir.
  7. Season to taste.

This is one of the recipies my dad always made when I was a kid. He would come home from work late at night and just start cooking! We'd be eating dinner at 1 in the morning. Fun times!
